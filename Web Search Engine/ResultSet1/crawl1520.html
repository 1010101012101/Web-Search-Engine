We're one of the top journalism departments in the country for teaching and research. Our courses are about creating multimedia journalists with high ethical standards, drive and real experience of the professional world. Our graduates are journalists of the future – influential thinkers who thrive on change.

Our undergraduate degree is one of only a few journalism courses in the country to be accredited by all three industry bodies. Our vocational postgraduate courses are all industry accredited.

 

Undergraduate journalism degrees

We'll teach you about every aspect of journalism – TV, radio, web, newspapers and magazines. You'll join a buzzing, creative team and get experience of the professional world.
