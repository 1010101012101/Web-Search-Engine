Local Media Facts

There are 1,100 local newspapers and 1,600 associated websites in the UK.

The local press is the UK’s most popular print medium, read by 31 million people a week.

Local media websites attract 62 million unique users each month.

Over 60% of people act on the ads in local newspapers.

Local newspapers are more than twice as trusted as any other media channel.

Local media employs 30,000 people including 10,000 journalists.
