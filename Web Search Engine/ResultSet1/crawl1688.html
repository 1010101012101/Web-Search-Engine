Track News, Inc. is a News Monitor.  We archive most types of media in our broadcast library, including Radio, and TV stations.  We have the capabilities to do caption text research in 100 plus markets (DMA's) around the country.  We provide a way for you to locate a video dub,  Transcript of a News clip, Media Report, a copy or copies of old News Stories from our Archives, a National Tracking service, and a Story hit alert system. We specialize in Legal research for Attorneys/Law Firms, Crisis management for Public Relation departments, and special events for PR Firms. Additionally, Track has created a Database for our members  giving detailed information about Markets, DMA(s), Viewer Numbers, Advertising costs, Channel coverage,  Monitoring availability, and contact info for key personnel at Broadcast Stations.
