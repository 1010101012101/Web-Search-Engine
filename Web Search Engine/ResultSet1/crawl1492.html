Free journalism training modules - help yourself

Welcome to Media Helping Media, a free training resource available for anyone to download and use. Media Helping Media was set up in 2005, and has more than 100 free training modules. Many have been translated and the material used in journalism training worldwide. Most of the content on this site is released under Creative Commons BY-NC-SA.

Training modules used worldwide

Available for all to use

Media Helping Media has training modules listed in 17 categories including:

  • Basic Journalism Training
  • Advanced Journalism Training
  • Editorial Ethics
  • Investigative Journalism
  • Data Journalism
  • Online Journalism & Multimedia
  • Media Management
  • Media Strategy
  • Training Of Trainers

About Media Helping Media

Media Helping Media has no financial backers. The site is independent and free from any outside influence. You can find out more in our About Us page. All income from the Google adverts running on this site goes to Kiva in order to fund loans to help build sustainable businesses in the developing world.
