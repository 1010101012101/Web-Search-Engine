You don't find many love letters
lying on the side of the road.
But this one was.
Read it below.
It was written in blue ink and pencil
on a piece of ruled notebook paper.


dated May 29, 1996

Chris,

Hey baby what's up? Well here not much, just here thinking about you like always! Babe, I really really really love you with all my heart! And I promise I won't cheat behind your back. I could never be with anyone else besides you.

I promise that every chance I get I'll write to you, okay? Man, babe, I don't think I could last 8 weeks without you, I can't even last 1 second wihthout you. No one else could ever take your place. Babe, please try to keep out of trouble while I'm gone. I don't want anything to happen to you!

Take care of yourself. I really don't want you drinking, but you probably will since I won't be here to know what you're doing. Babe, I never want to loose (sic) you, that's why I don't like for you to drink. I'm really really going to miss you!

dated May 30, 1996

Well babe, I guess I won't be writing to you those kinds of stuff because after what happened last night. Babe, if you really want to break-up with me just tell me. Like I said before, I never want to loose (sic) you.

You mean everything in the world to me. Last night I stayed up all night long thinking about what I would do without you. To tell you the truth I really didn't know if it was real or just a dream, I really wished it was a dream. Chris, while I'm in San Marcos, you could do whatever you want (I really don't want you to but I'm not going to be here to konw what you're doing).

I really don't write nice in pencil so I'll write in pen now. Chris, if you are going t be with another girk, make sure I don't find out. Okay? And I promise I'll never be with another guy again. I thought seeing you with another girl hurt me but when you you told me you wanted to break-up with me, that hurt me the most. I'm so sorry. I should have told you the truth. But like I said if you want to break up then tell me before I leave. I never thought I would fall in love with you. I'm so glad you gave me an extra chance. I don't konw what I would have done if you broke-up with me.

Well, I don't want to bore you anymroe than what I already did, so bye!

Love always,

Yvette
