9-month-old girl dies in West Side apartment

Updated: 2013-12-11 21:45:01

A 9-month-old girl has been pronounced dead after she was found unresponsive in a West Side apartment after a neighbor discovered her in a snowsuit wrapped in blankets while in a car seat, authorities said. The girl was found in the apartment at 3:37 p.m. Wednesday in the 3700 block of West 13th Street, authorities said. An older sibling of the girl went to a neighbor seeking food because there was none in the apartment, and then the neighbor discovered the 9-month-old inside.
