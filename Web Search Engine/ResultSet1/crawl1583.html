Southern Utah News Front Page: December 11, 2013

Fundraiser for Jayci Glover on Dec. 16

Jayci Glover gets a hug from her favorite country music singer Luke Bryan backstage after his concert.

 

Jayci Glover is the 12-year-old daughter of Coby and Heather Glover, and the granddaughter of Danny and Leslie Glover and Harold and Cynthia Hamblin. For the past year, she has been battling a very rare and aggressive form of cancer, Peripheral T-Cell Lymphoma.

She has been in Primary Children’s Hospital, and this past July she underwent an Autologous Bone Marrow (her own bone marrow) transplant. The transplant was not successful in knocking out the cancer. She is now back at Primary’s preparing for an allogeneic transplant (bone marrow donor). She has gone through the first round of chemo and will require a second round. Doctors are hopeful that she will be able to have the donor transplant in January.

Jayci’s family is fighting right along side her, and are doing everything they can for her. Jayci is a fighter! She has shown amazing strength and spirit.

Please join us at the fundraising events being held on Monday, December 16, at 6:30 p.m. at Kanab High School. We will be holding a silent auction for one half beef (cut to winner’s specifications) from Kanab Custom Meats, and a large western-style Minky Quilt donated by Corinne Jensen. We will be holding over 40 different raffles, including custom cabinets by Hutchings Cabinets valued at $950, 100 gallons of propane from Amerigas, a $250 gift certificate from Randy’s Auto Body, over $175 in gift certificates to Head 2 Toe Salon, $150 in gift cards from Zion Pharmacy, various gift certificates from Tara Thackeray (Kanab Spray Tan), Xpressweb, Subway, LaBella Salon, Best Western, Comfort Inn, Spurs Grill, Charlie’s Oasis, Parry Lodge, Rodeway Inn, Victorian Inn, Amerigas, Alicia Cram Photography, Jaymelyn Photography, Michelle Federkeil Photography, Escobar’s, Duke’s, Samco, Adobe Fitness, Josue Rivera (personal training), Pizza Hut, and gifts from Canyon Book, Lumber Plus, Barry Glazier, Sherwoods Sew That, Red Rock Apiaries, Stampin’ Up, Carly Grymes (DoTerra Oils), Cheryl Tullis (Scentsy), Monica Croteau (Thirty One), Camille Johnson (Lilacs & Jacks), Cedar Trading Post, Robyn Rogers, JoAnn Barnson, Kanab United Drug, Kanab Furniture, Garkane, and more.

Then join us for a delicious Houston’s Chicken Fried Chicken Dinner!

Come out and support this amazing girl and her family! If you can’t make it, you can still donate to the Jayci Glover Fund at State Bank of Southern Utah.
