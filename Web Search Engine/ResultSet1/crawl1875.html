Welcome

The Mississippi Press Association is the trade group for over 120 member newspapers in the State of Mississippi. To learn more about our Association and newspapers in the Magnolia State, use the links in the left-hand navigation column.

7-in-10 Mississippians read their local paper

A new study finds that 7-in-10 Mississippians, or 1.5 million adults, are regular newspaper readers. Further, newspapers prove to be the most vital source for shopping information among Mississippi consumers.

Read more about the 2013 Advertising and Media Use in Mississippi survey and find out more about the strength of newspapers in state and nationwide.
