Welcome to Zenark - the Home of Web Intelligence

Zenark is the company behind Electric Search, the UK & Ireland's leading web intelligence and media monitoring solution.

Zenark provides its clients with news, information, and opinion from every key online source that impacts their business. With critical information collected and delivered to them in a timely fashion, decision-makers can spot threats and opportunities and act immediately and effectively while saving hours of manual research. This has changed the way in which our clients keep on top of news stories.

Over 70% of the UK & Ireland's business population turn first to the web for breaking news stories. Electric Search is trusted by hundreds of organisations to track stories online, monitor the effectiveness of PR and lobbying campaigns, and protect their brands and reputations.
