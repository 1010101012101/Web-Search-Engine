These pages provide links to United States news archives available on the Web.

  • Sources are arranged by state. Link to the home page or the "search archives" page.
  • Use the browser's FIND button to find a source in this list.
  • We provide the dates of the archive, along with the cost to retrieve the item. Unless noted, searching is free. Charges may apply to retrieval.
  • Archives of international, Canadian, and Asian news sources are also available.
  • If you don't find what you're looking for, try one of our Other Sources.
  • The Special Libraries Association News Division Web Team maintains this list of links to news archives. We do not have any special access privileges to the archives. We cannot do research for inquirers. If you seek material from a specific source and have problems finding the material online, contacting that source directly might be the best option.

These pages are maintained by volunteers of the Special Libraries Association News Division.
We are looking for a new volunteer to maintain these pages. If you are interested, please contact Alice Pepper

Send additions or corrections to this page to Alice Pepper. If you're suggesting an archive, please include the URL of the newspaper, the URL of the archive, and pricing information.
