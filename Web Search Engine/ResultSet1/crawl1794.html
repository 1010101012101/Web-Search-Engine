Superior, Comprehensive News Monitoring

Headquartered in Austin, but with offices throughout Texas, Teleclip, Inc has provided superior broadcast news monitoring and news media coverage since 1978 in the Austin area and beyond. Though our roots are in Texas, we have expanded in recent years to cover global markets as well as online news mediums.

News Radio Monitoring Expands at Teleclip

Teleclip expands radio monitoring by adding another 21,000 hours of regularly indexed and searchable programming in over 100 U.S. markets

March 01, 2013 – Utilizing advanced speech-to-text technology, Teleclip has expanded regularly indexed and searchable radio content to include an additional 100 U.S. markets with over 21,000 hours of programming.  This is an addition to Teleclip’s comprehensive TV, print, online monitoring, content management and analysis solutions.

Our clients are now able to search, report and clip radio coverage in near real time from markets and stations that have never been indexed before. Clients will now be able to report more radio coverage than ever before and our expansion of radio recordings and indexing is growing monthly.

Teleclip is affiliated with NDS, a corporation comprised of over 30 national and regional media monitoring companies that combine their resources to provide the deepest national media monitoring solutions. NDS affiliates are among some of the most established media monitoring companies in the world. NDS provides comprehensive monitoring (TV, radio, print, online news, social media), reporting, measurement and analysis, along with easy-to-use tools, executive briefing services, and expert customer service.

Teleclip adds new Power Search and EditClip video service

Our collaboration with NDS allows us to offer our newest client services – Power Search and EditClip.  Both are subscription services that provide clients with an executive dashboard portal allowing users to search for any video by date, market, station and newscast.

EditClip provides client instant capabilities to view a clip, edit the video and permanently archive that video segment on the clients online dashboard portal.  EditClip feature subscribers have the option to select the type of video format that is best suited for their needs, whether it be high-resolution or low-resolution WMV, Flash video, MPEG, MOV video files.
