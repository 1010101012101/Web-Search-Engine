Man Miraculously Recovers from Severe Stroke

Yesterday God demonstrated his divine power when Jacob Ladder instantly recovered from a debilitating stroke that left him paralyzed just over one year ago. Jacob was told by doctors that he would spend the rest of his life confined to a wheel chair as his left side was completely paralysed from shoulder to heel. After Jacob’s stroke, he was unable to speak clearly and could only make uttering sounds.
Jacob was part of over one hundred Korean's that packed Missions Evangelical Church in South Korea.

Woman's Life Saved in Life Threatening Car Accident

Two days ago Amy Bloom was traveling down I90 just outside of Toledo when she lost control of her car and was hit head on by a transport truck. To the bystanders at the scene there was "no way anyone could have survived that car crash".
The driver of the truck was taken to Sunnybrook Hospital with minor injuries and to everyone’s amazement Amy was fund pinned in her vehicle with only minor injuries until emergency response arrived.
"I remember I felt such peace as I lost control of my car and headed right into oncoming traffic" Remarked Amy Bloom as she recounted some of the traumatic moments of the accident.

Revival Starts in Brazil

Last week marked the beginning of a Crusade where over 350 lives were saved. This crusade being led by Mark Waters is the first of its kind in the Matto Grotto province in Central Brazil. Over 1000 people gathered in the Missions Evangelical Church to worship and praise Jesus Christ and while doing so the Holy Spirit descended upon the masses. Within an hour, people by the hundreds were being healed and restored in the flesh. The deaf began to hear and the blind also began to see.

"I have been waiting all of my life for something like this" remarked Amy Stern, one of the organizers of the event.
