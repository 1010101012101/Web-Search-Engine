APN News & Media has a diverse portfolio of vibrant media assets.

APN has successful outdoor advertising businesses in Australia and New Zealand and has strong growing operations in Hong Kong.

In New Zealand, we have three of the top five national radio networks with NewsTalk ZB, Classic Hits and Coast as well as four additional major networks. In Australia, our major metro radio networks are Mix and Classic Hits connecting audiences in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane and Adelaide. We also operate The Edge in Sydney.

APN publishes 20 daily and more than 100 non-daily newspapers across Australia and New Zealand. We are proud to be the leading publisher in New Zealand, with The New Zealand Herald spearheading public opinion in the country. In Australia, we focus on providing relevant and engaging publications in some of Australia’s fastest growing regions.

With market leading online sites such as NZ Herald Online and GrabOne and a strong audience connection with our radio and news branded web and mobile sites, APN is expanding its digital potential.

APN also has large commercial printing businesses in Australia and New Zealand.
