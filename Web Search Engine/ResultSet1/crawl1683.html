Take Control Today

Take control today. Smart media monitoring prepares you for the press and puts you in control of your coverage.

That’s why J&A delivers relevant information that matters to you. Used to manually filtering irrelevant news stories? Well, you won’t have to do that anymore – ever. Because we send just the updates you need. No more. No less.

We monitor more media sources than any other Canadian firm. Since 1989, we’ve helped public and private Canadian companies, the three levels of government, associations, and PR firms monitor critical media developments and improve strategic communications.

It’s a service powered by people – not automation – and it’s as comprehensive as they come. Here’s what you get:

  • Relevant information: Our intelligent approach gives you just the information you need. Everything we send you is relevant and ready for action.
  • Experienced people: With over 20 years in the business, our team offers unmatched media monitoring expertise.
  • Comprehensive coverage: TV, radio, print, Internet, social media. We cover all media groups from all regions of Canada.
  • Reports that perform: With ready-to-use reports customized to your preferences, we show you every pertinent issue in just one place.

At J&A, we take the hassle out of media monitoring. We send just what you need, right when you need it – so you can finally take control.
