About:

 

I have been an active user of the Internet since 1994; a time when many individuals were still unfamiliar with services of the internet including: email and the World Wide Web. Since then, my skills have broadened greatly, gaining strong experience in multiple disciplines of information technology.

 

I specialize in web application development and database integration, focusing on implementing open source software and technologies, particularly Content Management System (CMS) integration and customization built on LAMP framework. In addition to being a technology enthusiast, I have a strong background in web accessibility, usability and graphic design, internet technology, and open source-based information technology infrastructure management solutions.

 

My services include: web design, web development, database, script, programming, logo and banner design, site promotion, search engine optimization, marketing, shared and dedicated managed web site hosting, and system administration. In short, I provide a turnkey solution for your needs whether you are an individual looking to establish on-line presence, a non-profit, a company, a corporation, and/or a non-governmental organization.

 

I have worked with and continue to work with corporate, non-profit, non-governmental organizations since 1998 as a freelancer, a consultant, and as a full time employee.
