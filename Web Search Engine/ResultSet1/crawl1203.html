NEW DVD NOW AVAILABLE!

ROYAL CANADIAN AIR FARCE: 40 FOR 40

It was on December 9, 1973, that CBC Radio broadcast the very first episode of ROYAL CANADIAN AIR FARCE. Twenty years later, on October 8, 1993, CBC Television brought AIR FARCE to a whole new audience.

To celebrate Air Farce's 40th anniversary, eOne has released a new DVD, ROYAL CANADIAN AIR FARCE: 40 FOR 40, featuring 40 of the most popular sketches from Air Farce's TV years.

There are also bonus features like classic radio sketches (some of them unheard in years) and rare video clips from the Air Farce archives!

It's an hilarious look back at some of your most-requested favourites, so if you're looking for a few laughs, or would like to do some early holiday shopping, visit your preferred online retailer (including CBCShop.ca, Amazon.ca, Indigo.ca, HMV.ca, or Walmart.ca) to pick up a copy!

 

CLASSIC AIR FARCE MOMENTS AVAILABLE ONLINE FOR FREE!

The new AIR FARCE FUNNIES! page posts brand-new compilation episodes, pulled from our 350-plus TV shows and specials that have aired on CBC since 1993.

Each webisode is filled with sketches grouped together with a special theme, and features a new introduction by Don Ferguson.

Anytime you're looking for a few laughs from Roger, Don, Luba, John, Jessica, Craig, Alan, and Penelope, feel free to drop by and click around. Re-watch your favourites or discover sketches you may have missed the first time around!
