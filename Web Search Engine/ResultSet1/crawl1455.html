9th Circuit Court of Appeals Upholds Statutory Ban on Public Stations Airing Paid Advertisements

A panel of eleven judges sitting on the United State Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, in effect, overrode a panel of three judges on the same Court, and sided with the United States District Court for the Northern District of California which had upheld as constitutional the statutory prohibition against public radio and television stations airing paid advertisements for for-profit entities, for issues of public importance or interest, and for political candidates.

Distinguished Service Award

Dave Putnam (i), former IBA President presented the Distinguished Service Award to Congressman Leonard Boswell (c) at the Iowa Broadcasters Association Summer Convention on June 26, 2013. Joel McRea (r), assisted in the presentation.

 

Hall of Fame

Daniel Miller (r), former General Manager of Iowa Public Television, was inducted into the Iowa Broadcasters Hall of Fame at the IBA Summer Convention, June 26, 2013. Dave Putnam (l), former IBA President, presented Miller with the Hall of Fame Award.

 

Broadcaster of the Year

Congratulations to Michael Elrod (r), General Manager of KYOU-TV, who was chosen as the 2013 Iowa Broadcasters Association Broadcaster of the Year. Elrod was presented the Broadcaster of the Year award by former IBA President Dave Putnam (l) at the IBA Summer Convention, June 26, 2013, in West Des Moines.
