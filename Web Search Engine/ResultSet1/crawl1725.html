Cut and Paste

By Anne Cassidy

The other day I was revising an essay, moving paragraphs around with keystrokes and trying to keep the entire structure in mind. It was my usual process -- except that it wasn't working. The piece was a muddle. I had lost my way. What I wanted was to see the article in its entirety. More »
