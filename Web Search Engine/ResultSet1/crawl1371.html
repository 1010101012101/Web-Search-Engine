Bringing More Than 150 Years of Experience to the Web

Reaching more than 35 million unique visitors and serving more than 320 million page views monthly.

Omniture - January 2013

McClatchy Interactive serves as the interactive hub for The McClatchy Company, the third-largest newspaper company in the United States. Working hand-in-hand with 30 daily newspapers, it is our mission to help establish each one as the leading online source of local news within their respective markets. McClatchy Interactive also supports dozens of non-dailies, as well as an array of niche sites.
