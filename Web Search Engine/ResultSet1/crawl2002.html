Welcome to the Sierra Sage!

The Magazine
The Sierra Sage will be undergoing some major changes in the near future and will be going to an electronic format only. This has been planned for sometime (as you know) but "burn-out" and other duties have kept it on the back burner. It's time to move it to the front! The first stage will bring back our famous Sierra Sage commentary. With the events going on around us, some "reason, justice and common sense" perspective is sorely needed!

Next will be format changes with special "page flipping" software. This will allow us to publish online and and download to iPads, iPhones and other smart devices. Kindle format is coming. Current subscribers will have full access to the new formats and new web users will be required to subscribe to receive the full content. 

A WEEKLY WEB UPDATE will offer more regular commentary and will be published to the website each Monday. 

The Book
The 1st edition of Len Semas's book, Reason, Justice and Common Sense, (foreword by Barry Goldwater, Jr.) is SOLD OUT. An expanded and revised 2nd edition featuring 8-10 new chapters is in process and expected to be released in mid 2013 as an eBook (iPad, Kindle).
