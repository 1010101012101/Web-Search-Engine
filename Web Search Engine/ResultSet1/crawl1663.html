What we do

Companies use Apollo to measure and improve the success of their communications. Specializing in the technology, services and electronics sectors, Apollo’s unique research program analyzes media coverage across the US, Europe and Asia. Today, over 200 leading companies and PR agencies throughout the US and Europe use our simple but powerful reports and online tools. The results give your share of voice, your position within key thought-leadership topics, influencers that need to be targeted, sentiment and messages.

Influencers

It’s never been so important to target the right influencers. It’s never been so hard. Apollo’s research shows technology companies are having to contend with the three Vs when they target influencers. The variety of influence has increased to include journalists, analysts, vendors, end-users, systems integrators, academics and more. At the same time, the volume of influencers has grown, as has the velocity at which many of them work and move around.

The Apollo Rankings

Every month Apollo analyzes over 250,000 online and print articles on technology in 27 countries. Each month we publish the top-line results of the research for 13 of the countries. The results provide a broad overview of the companies, journalists, analysts and topics that are most prominent on the media's agenda in each country. This is very much a top-line view of how the media are covering the technology industry.
