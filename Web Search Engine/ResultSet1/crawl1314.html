CCC Will Offer Courses that Promote Cross-Cultural Awareness
Posted: Thursday, November 07, 2013

Corning Community College (CCC) faculty will offer courses in partnership with international faculty in 2014-2015 through The State University of New York (SUNY) Center for Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL). COIL courses provide a unique opportunity for CCC students that promote cross-cultural awareness. Classes have a blended format with traditional face-to-face sessions taking place separately at CCC and the partnering, international institution, while collaborative student work takes place online.
