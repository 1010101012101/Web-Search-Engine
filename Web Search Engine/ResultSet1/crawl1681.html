Welcome to UniversalJournal.org, the official site of The Association of Young Journalists and Writers. Aimed to give real world experience and to open job opportunities for the journalists of tomorrow, the non-profit organization provides a vast number of opportunities, resources, awards, and other beneficial tools for the starting journalist. You may start your visit by checking out some of the links to the left or scroll down for a complete list of what this site has to offer.


Get Published - Have your articles, essays, poems, or any other writing posted in our vast library of writing available for over 6,000 newspapers and magazines to parse, buy, and publish.

Read Articles - Read fascinating articles, essays, and other writings by aspiring journalists in the fields of Politics, Science, Technology, Business, Travel, Life/People, Health, Education, Arts & Entertainment, Law, Literature, and Sports.

Submit Your Writing - Use our online word processor to type up and work on your writing while surrounded by links and tips to improve your writing as you go. However, if you prefer to work on your own system, you can copy/paste your work into our entry to submit it.

Find Media - Search our extensive database of media throughout the United States and Canada, giving you not only the locations of media but also their websites. The directory contains over 6,000 listings, consisting of newspapers, magazines, radio, tv, and online media.

Colleges With Journalism Programs - View our directory of colleges throughout the United States that offer journalism studies.

Free Classifieds - Read and post classified ads of any topic for free.

Free Resumes - Read and post resumes for media jobs or any other.

Play Intellectual Games - Play games that challenge the mind for free. You don't even have to download them. Play now and test your skills.
