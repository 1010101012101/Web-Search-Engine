The Truth Is In Here
Aquarius
After the 20th you will have an irrational urge to roast chestnuts on an open fire. This is perfectly normal for the time of year, however, if Jack Frost is also nipping at your nose check you have not slipped into a Star Trek Generations type parallel universe by firmly squeezing tree located baubles in department stores. Aries
It's a nose-to-the-grindstone month this month, although, of course, as a bossy Aries-ian, the nose doesn't necessarily have to be yours. [More]
Cancer
Latin American dictators will, at times, make things awkward this month, especially ones that speak in Spanish and point violently when delivering their speeches. Be particularly wary of South American related commemorative plates which arrive unexpectedly in the post. [More]
Capricorn
A moment of 1970's boy band, Osmond Brother based, nostalgia, will excite your interest in white all-in-one suits with coloured lapels. [More]
Gemini
This month, raw apple, lemon, oranges, mango, passion fruit, kiwi, strawberries, raspberries, grapefruit, but not cranberries, are out of favour with most of the planets. Conversely, pies or cakes which include these fruits as an ingredient are plumply starred. [More]
Leo
A visit to a zoo, a leisure park, or a sleazy motel, will all end messily. Ensure bottled water is on hand and a rag to mop your furrowed brow. [More]
Libra
Over the Christmas season you will see a movie and review it completely wrongly to friends at a social occasion. Your overheard comments will be deemed so newsworthy that your review will be mentioned mockingly on a local radio breakfast show. [More]
Pisces
As a pie loving Piscean you will be emotionally overcome this month with the depth and breadth of pie based creativity in the universe. You will be surrounded by pies filled with apple, loganberry, mango and sweet mince meat, which are set to make this month zing in a similar way the swinging 60's did with highly coloured clothing and shoes. [More]
Sagittarius
Your skills as an impersonator will provide your friends with moments of mirth this month as, for reasons known only to the gilding on Mars's upper-cusp, the planets have contrived to get the person you are sending up to sneak up on you from behind in mid impersonation. [More]
Scorpio
An argument with an advocate of Intelligent Design, whom you meet in a restaurant around the 19th, is set to provide moments of high drama on your spiritual debating plane for most of the month. The planets will contrive to fill your head with ludicrous Big Bang arguments as a Christmas season treat for them. [More]
Taurus
A contract which gave Microsoft rights over all creative work authored by you while using their software (which you agreed to when you clicked Agree without reading all of the clauses when installing Microsoft software in 2006) is set to reduce your income this month by over a third. Let this be a lesson to you next time a drinks fuelled argument breaks out in which you take Microsoft's side. [More]
Virgo
Soft chewy toffees and baby dogs are badly matched this month, unless you want to do that thing where you put toffee around a mutt's mouth so that when the dog licks his own lips you pretend they are talking by throwing your voice. Practice your ventriliquisting skills during dull moments as there is a 20% chance you could appear on local television after your dog enchants an executive's child. [More]
