Rag Box Satirical Commentary

Comments gleaned from various news sources are satirical in nature. Everything here is coincidental. A lot of it is made up, even though the stories are based on readily available news articles. Satire naturally involves literary license. So get over it if you have a problem with it. The stories written here are opinions. As such, they do not necessarily reflect facts. But they may be based on factual material readily available to any reader. Others are more personal in nature, but those stories are even more naturally opinions. Hopefully, you understand the differences. If you would like to submit an article for review, send it to gvr@cfl.rr.com. Rag Box reserves the right to refuse any articles. If they contain sexual, or other innapropriate or prurient content, they will be bounced either to the sender or to the authorities. If it's good political or entertainment satire, send it. We also reserve the right to make grammatical/spelling changes if necessary. Your name will appear as the author. This will be your claim, and only claim to fame. But you can at least say that you write for an online newspaper. We'll even provide a killer letter of reference. Think about it. It's all about the resume.
