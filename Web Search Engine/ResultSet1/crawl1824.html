A Message from Media Source President Joe Incardona

We offer a full range of corporate and broadcast video production services.  Our 5000-square foot facility includes 2400 square feet of studio space, multiple HD edit suites, and a complement of HD cameras. 

Media Source is also a member of News Data Services, a nationwide system of media monitoring services that cover all 210 US media markets and all national cable and satellite TV networks.  We offer comprehensive media monitoring services via our Web-based media dashboard, and can tailor a plan to fit your specific public relations and media tracking needs.

 

New at Media Source: "The Spark" TV Show on WKNO-TV PBS Channel 10

We are proud to be the production team behind The Spark, an innovative new 30-minute show that showcases the positive impact the business community is having on Memphis and the Mid-South.  The Spark is a collaborative effort of Media Source, the Lipscomb & Pitts Breakfast Club, and WKNO-TV, the PBS affiliate for Memphis and the Mid-South.  The show airs every third Thursday of the month at 9 PM on WKNO Channel 10.1, and has multiple repeat broadcasts on WKNO 10.1 and 10.2.
