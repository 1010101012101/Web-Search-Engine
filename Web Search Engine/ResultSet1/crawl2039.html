Parkston School Board to meet Dec. 16
The Parkston School Board will meet Monday, Dec. 16, at 6:30 p.m. in the Elementary Conference room.
Preliminary agenda items include:
Call to order; establish a quorum; Pledge of Allegiance; approve agenda; approve consent agenda; approve minutes; accept financial reports; approve claims visitors and correspondence; Superintendent’s report; Principal and Athletic Director Reports; old business-food service discussion, final reading of physical force policy; new business-ASBSD resolution discussion, identify window to determine FTE, surplus library books, request for recognition; executive session-negotiations, personnel, approve negotiated agreement; and adjournment.
The Parkston School Board wants to foster community feedback and input. Anyone who wishes to talk to the board about any topic is encouraged to do so. A place on the agenda is reserved for such conversations under ‘visitor and correspondence.’ The Board asks that you contact the superintendent in advance so that necessary arrangements, if required, can be made.
