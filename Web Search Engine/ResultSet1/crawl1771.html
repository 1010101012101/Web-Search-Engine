Panarc International

1 Issue Analysis

Panarc’s Issue Analysis consultancy service is designed to help government and companies in:

  • dissecting crucial issues,
  • understanding how these issues are perceived by the media and the public and
  • dealing with any problems in an effective way.

In the last 30 years, Panarc has provided media coverage or issue analysis services to most UK government departments, many leading UK companies and a number of foreign governments.

As an adjunct to Issue Analysis, Panarc maintains a separate website, the Third Rock Forum. Our purpose is to provide intelligent, thoughtful and concerned individuals anywhere in the world with the opportunity to make constructive contributions to debates about local, national or global issues. Go to the Third Rock Forum website Forum (www.thirdrockforum.org).

2 Software

Over the last 30 years, Panarc has developed a number of software applications, mainly for its own internal use. Some of these applications will be of interest to other users. The first of these is DocuPraxis®, a programme designed to assist writers in the preparation of documents. Go to the Docupraxis® website (www.docupraxis.com)

3 Publishing

Panarc is the publisher of a limited number of selected titles in a variety of genres, both fiction and non-fiction, available from the Panarc Publishing website or from Amazon. Our aim is to publish books which explore complex subjects in an entertaining and accessible form. Go to the Panarc Publishing website (www.panarcpublishing.com)
