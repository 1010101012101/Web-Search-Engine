The CPU Media Trust is pledged to uphold and defend the freedom, interests and welfare of the media in Commonwealth countries. It will actively promote the highest professional and ethical standards and will monitor and oppose any measures designed to affect the freedom of the press and of journalists in any member state of the Commonwealth.
