Discoveries and Breakthroughs Inside Science

AIP's syndicated television news service providing accurate, informative and entertaining news covering everything from astronomy to zoology.

DBIS home | About | Partners | Stations | Contact us | Information for Partners

Welcome to the web archive for AIP's Discoveries and Breakthroughs Inside Science program, which ran from 2000-2011. We hope you enjoy the selected content from the program on this site. The program created over 1200 videos during its 11-year run and was broadcast in television stations all over the United States.

In January 2012, the program was succeeded by Inside Science TV (ISTV). With an updated format and look, ISTV produces short-form news segments about cutting-edge research and development in science, engineering, and mathematics designed to fit seamlessly into local television newscasts in markets across the United States and internationally.
