UH Students' Plan Aims to Revitalize Houston's Kuhlman Gully

December 11, 2013 - Longtime residents of the community have memories of what Kuhlman Gully used to look like. If two University of Houston architecture students have their way, the gully may start to resemble the natural gem it once was.
Mike Emery

UH Moores Opera Center Earns National Honors for Third Straight Year

December 10, 2013 - Audiences in Houston are well aware that the University of Houston boasts an ambitious and entertaining opera program. Following recent awards from the National Opera Association (NOA), opera aficionados around the country are starting to discover what local audiences have known all along.
Mike Emery

Four UH Researchers Named to National Academy of Inventors

December 10, 2013 - Four UH researchers have been named as fellows to the National Academy of Inventors, driven both by intellectual curiosity and by the desire to improve the lives of the people around them.
Jeannie Kever

UH Physicist Honored as Rising Star in Texas Research

December 10, 2013 - Zhifeng Ren received the O'Donnell Award in science from The Academy of Medicine, Engineering and Science of Texas, which cited his contributions in five scientific fields: carbon nanotubes, thermoelectrics, hierarchical zinc oxide nanowires, high temperature superconductivity, and molecule delivery/sensing.
Jeannie Kever

Expedition Yields Unexpected Clues to Ocean Mysteries

December 03, 2013 - A UH geoscientist and his colleagues reveal a few surprises they discovered upon penetrating the lower crust of the Pacific Ocean. The findings from this $10 million international expedition are described in the Dec. 1 issue of Nature.
Lisa Merkl

Noted Branding, Marketing, Public Relations Leader Joins UH, UH System

December 02, 2013 - Richie Hunter, a well-recognized leader in strategic marketing, brand development, integrated communications and business strategy, has joined the University of Houston as associate vice president/associate vice chancellor for marketing and communication for the University and the UH System.
Richard Bonnin
