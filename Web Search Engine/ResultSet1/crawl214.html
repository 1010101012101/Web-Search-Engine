About ACP

In 1921, the Associated Collegiate Press, a division of the National Scholastic Press Association began helping college journalists and advisers improve their publications. Today that goal remains No. 1. For more information, visit the About Us page or the ACP FAQ.

Sponsorship opportunities

ACP's exhibitors, advertisers and sponsors help make many of our activities possible. For information about how NSPA and ACP can help get your message out to the high school and college press, visit our sponsor page.
